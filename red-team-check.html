Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Red Team Skill Assessment.

What is a Red Team Skill Assessment? +
A hands-on test of real-world red team attack skills across Active Directory and Azure.
Is this red team assessment beginner-friendly? +
Yes, the assessment is suitable for beginners as well as experienced professionals.
Do I need prior experience to take this test? +
Basic knowledge of active directory or azure is helpful, but not mandatory. The assessment is designed to identify your current skill level.
How long does the skill assessment take? +
The assessment typically takes around 5–10 minutes to complete depending on your pace.
What happens after I complete the assessment? +
You receive a skill score and unlock access to hands-on Red Team labs tailored to your level.
Will I get access to Red Team labs? +
Yes, completing the assessment unlocks access to enterprise-grade Red Team labs for practice.
Is the Red Team Skill Check free? +
Yes, the skill assessment is free and gives you access to red team labs.
What topics are covered in the assessment? +
Topics include Active Directory attacks, Azure security, privilege escalation, lateral movement, and post-exploitation techniques.
